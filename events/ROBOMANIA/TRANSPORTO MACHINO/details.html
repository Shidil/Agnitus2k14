Engineering is the branch of science that deals with creating shortcuts for the laziest of living beings -the humans!! Man, from the time wheel was invented, is trying to simplify efforts to accomplish a task. Their quest for the same has led to invention of robots. We at Agnitus salute this laziness that has led to many inventions that make our life convenient!!! So, it’s time to create your own bot to help you in a pick and place task. [b]Problem Statement[/b] With the aid of an autonomous or remote controlled (could be wired) machine, pick the blocks given, carry and place it at the destination within the specified arena in the shortest span of time. [b]Block Specification[/b] There will be 10 cylindrical wooden blocks, each having a base diameter of 4 cm and height 8 cm, and an approximate weight of 150 g (within 10% error). All blocks will have a metallic semi-circular ring (of non-magnetic material) on the top.
