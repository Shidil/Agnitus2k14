[b] INTRODUCTION[/b][br/] Agnitus 2k13 provides you a space to play with circuits in the mind-boggling Circuit Contrivance, wherein the most important talent of an electronics engineer is put to test- the ability to design circuits. The final circuit is to be simulated in Proteus. 1st Prize: 8000/- 2nd Prize: 5000/- 3rd Prize: 3000/- Participants must have sufficient knowledge in working with proteus (simulation software). There will be a maximum of two members in a team. All applicants will go through a 20 minute elimination round. Participants are required to design a circuit for a given output on spot and simulate the same in Proteus. Decision of Judges will be final and cannot be questioned. There will be no limitation in the no. of team from a college. Cross-college teams are aslo allowed. Agnitus programme committee will not be responsible for any losses or harm during partcipation. Violating the rules and damaging the college property will attract penalty and permanent disqualification.
