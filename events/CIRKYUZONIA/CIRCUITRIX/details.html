[b] INTRODUCTION [/b][br/] Circuitrix is all about team work, intellect, patience and speed. This thrilling task delves into the world of tricky electronic circuitry wherein the participants are required to find out hidden faults in the given electrical circuit, and complete the different assigned tasks in the least possible time. 1st Prize: 8000/- 2nd Prize: 5000/- 3rd Prize: 3000/- There will be a maximum of two members in a team. Three blocks will be given with interconnected outputs. The objective is to get the output of the blocks in correct order in the least time while strictly following the Vertical hierarchy. Each team will be given a maximum of 22 minutes. There will be no limitation in the no. of teams from a college. Cross-college teams are aslo allowed.
